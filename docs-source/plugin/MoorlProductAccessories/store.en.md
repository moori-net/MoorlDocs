Shopware 6 plugin for accessories, product bundles and configurators: assign accessories flexibly, price them individually, and sell them directly in the storefront.

## Increase revenue with smart accessories

Increase your revenue with smart accessory and bundle selling in Shopware 6. This plugin allows you to enhance your products with matching add-ons that customers can select directly on the product detail page.

Accessories can be assigned flexibly to products or dynamic product groups and structured into clear categories. Different selection types such as single selection, multiple selection, and image-based options ensure an intuitive user experience and a modern shopping journey.

Define individual pricing rules for accessory items: use the original price, fixed custom prices, or percentage-based prices derived from the main product. If desired, all selected items can be combined into a bundle in the shopping cart. This creates transparent offer structures and encourages the sale of additional products.

The plugin is ideal for shops that want to sell accessories, optional extensions, or complete product bundles in a professional and flexible way.

## Typical Use Cases

- Add-on products and complementary items
- Product bundles and configurators
- Accessory sales on product detail pages
- Individual upselling and cross-selling scenarios
